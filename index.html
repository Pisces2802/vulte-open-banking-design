Banking as a Service

Our vision is to collaboratively provide the underlying banking services
infrastructure to fintechs and innovators in order to deliver best in class user
experiences and target the next generation bank customers.

All the APIs you need

Are you a fintech, developer or even an incumbent trying to do something new and novel? We want to collaborate with you, not compete.

Save your costs. Don’t rebuild what
exists already.

Whether you are looking for wallet infrastructure, or core banking
infrastructure to hold, manage and process deposits, with our APIs,
we turn banking to a service for you. That way, you can focus on
running your business.

Connect to a standardized gateway and switch providers as you see fit.

Options are everything. Whether it’s pricing or quality of service that’s the driving motivation, now you can switch between providers. Powered by our standardized APIs , we have given you the power to determine how your transaction is processed.

Earn revenue from value added services.

Be it TV payments, electricity payments, airtime purchase, or more… We provide APIs that let you offer these services and pay you a commission for every transaction.

Fight fraud and authenticate users with our KYC services.

With a combination of some of the extra APIs made available, you can fight fraud and create even more interesting user experiences. From APIs that lets you pull cards linked to a phone number, to APIs that let you tokenize cards, to APIs that show KYC details of account linked to a card and many more!

Loans as a service

Give loans, request loans. Your customers or ours. Doesn’t matter. We provide the APIs that let you assess risk, lookup statements, lien accounts, direct debit, and more. Basically, the APIs with which you need to run an effective digital lending business.

For developers, by developers.

For all the support you need, we have created a network of developers through community effort to provide you with all the support you need. We’re here.

Super advanced dashboards and business intelligence.

Detailed and advanced dashboards providing a good look at the health and progress of your business transactions
